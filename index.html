Meat Cut Quiz

Welcome to the Meat Cut Quiz! This application is designed to help you learn and identify different cuts of meat. You can choose between two types of quizzes:

  • Retail Quiz: Identify the retail cut names.
  • Primal Quiz: Identify the primal cuts.

Please make sure to enter your answers accurately. The quizzes will check your answers for case insensitivity and handle different types of spaces (full-width and half-width).
